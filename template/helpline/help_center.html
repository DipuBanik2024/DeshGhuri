Help Center

General Questions

What is DeshGhuri?

DeshGhuri is your travel companion platform, helping you explore destinations, find local guides, and plan unforgettable trips.

Do I need an account to explore destinations?

No, you can browse destinations freely. Creating an account allows you to save favorites and book tours.

For Travelers

How do I find local guides?

Go to our “Find Guides” page, enter your destination, and we’ll show verified guides available in that area.

Can I book tours online?

Yes, you can browse and book tours directly through our platform with secure payment options.

For Guides

How can I join DeshGhuri as a guide?

Create a guide profile, submit your credentials, and start receiving booking requests from travelers.

Is there a verification process?

Yes, guides are verified for identity, experience, and reviews to ensure traveler safety and quality service.

Still Need Help?

Email us at support@deshghuri.com or reach out via our Contact Form.
